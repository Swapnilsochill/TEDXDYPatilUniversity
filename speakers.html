TEDX SPEAKERS 2019

Teenasai Balamu

Teenasai Balamu, a singer and queer rights activist, fed up with being slotted into antiquated notions, decided to sing her way out of her misery.

Mithun Pangal

Mithun Pangal, innovator, inventor, and CTO of custom technologies, unveils his invaluable technique of breaking down complex problems in the simplest way imaginable.

Nilesh Kulkarni

>Nilesh Kulkarni, shares invaluable life-lessons from his time as a cricketer and talks at length about a spectacular career opportunity in sports after retirement.

Natasha Malpani

Mrs. Natasha Malpani Oswal, the founder of Dice Media, struggled to conform to the societal norms and her parents’ expectations to finally pursue her passion, from the horse’s mouth.

Muki Sablania

Muki Sablania, a hand-pan musician, a person who fashioned the hand-pan out of a gas cylinder because he couldn’t afford one.

Ashvin Kumar

Mr. Ashvin Kumar, a genius film-maker of Oscar-nominated film - Little Terrorist, encounters with the black reality of Kashmir form the basis of his thoughts.
TEDX SPEAKERS 2018

Kurambapatti Kids

These thirteen-year-olds and their classmates designed a cost-effective and practically viable toilet set-up which they called "Safe Mode Pissing System".

Suraj Jagan

Suraj Jagan, an Indian playback singer and frontman for rock bands like "Chakraview" & "Dream Out Loud", explains why is it important to love and accept yourself..

Diwakar Vaish

Prof. Diwakar Vaish, an Indian roboticist, introduces the world’s first cheapest and smallest ventilator that maneuvers artificial intelligence and is as big as an adult human palm.

Altaf Qadri

Altaf Qadri, a Diploma Engineer in Computers, tells a deeply personal story that impelled him into his profession and shows breathtaking images from his work.

Richa Singh

Richa Singh, the co-founder of Blogchatter, stresses the need to move on from looking out for instant gratification to using social media as a means of self-development and growth.

Pt. Sharad Dandge

Pt. Sharad Dandge, has incorporated the various cultures of India into sounds while he plays it on different Tablas around him.

Dr. Nandita Palshetkar

Dr. Nandita Palshetkar reveals that egg freezing is the solution for the existence of ordeals revolving around the biological clock for women.

Giri Balasubramaniam

Giri Balasubramaniam, the CEO of Greycaps, is a well know author and creator of several books and programs for schools across India.
